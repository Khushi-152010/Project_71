campus life

school gym/auditorium

was established in 2016

The school gym is very big. It is also used as the school auditorium as it has a stage and projector.

school library

was established in 2016

The school library is very big with over 500 books as well as 10 laptops for students to use! It also has a smart-board and lots of tables to work at.

school playground

was established in 2016

The school playground is very elaborate and vast. It is very fun to play in with all of the equipment the school provides.

school achievements

CT's Invention Convention
won first prize at connecticut's invention convention

At the Connecticut Invention Convention (a contest to see inventions and ideas of kids) our 5th grade students represented our school and our school won first prize with their cool inventions!(2018)

South Windsor Art Show
won first prize at the south windsor art show

At the South Windsor Art Show in the public library, a lot of students from our school entered and made it to the finals (including me) and I won first prize!(2017)

What's New

  • Virtual School Newspaper

    This school year, our student council met up each week and worked on making a virtual school newspaper where everyone can get updated. We chose for this newspaper to be virtual because of the pandemic and kids were learning online. So, we finished the website around January, 2021 and released it to the school.



  • K-12 Virtual Art Show

    Due to social distancing restrictions, the K-12 Art Show will be held virtually this year. In order to share and honor the works of art created by our students, an automated video presentation will be featured from May 17 - May 28.
